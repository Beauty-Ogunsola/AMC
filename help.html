To End Homelessness in America

We must strengthen our ability to prevent homelessness. To do that, we must take a multi-sector approach that focuses on housing needs, housing stability, and risks of homelessness across many different public systems. A Coordinated Approach. To end homelessness, a community-wide coordinated approach to delivering services, housing, and programs are needed. Housing is the Solution, Giving assistance for the Most Vulnerable and Increasing Employment and Income. Designing a Crisis Response for these situations.

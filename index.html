What causes Homelessness?


The main cause of homelessness is the lack of affordable housing, stagnant wages, unemployment, and healthcare costs that all play into being the root of homelessness.


The Statistics


HUD's 2020 Annual Homeless Assessment Report said that On a single night in January 2020, 580,466 people were experiencing homelessness in America. – about 18 of every 10,000 people in the United States – experienced homelessness across the United States. This represents a 2.2 percent increase from 2019.


Who are Affected?


In January 2020, there were 580,466 people experiencing homelessness in America. 70 percent were single individuals, and the rest were people living in families with children. They lived in every state and territory, and they reflected the diversity of our country.
